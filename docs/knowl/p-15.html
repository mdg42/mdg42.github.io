A common phrase when learning mathematics is that it is a language. In order to understand any subject, you must become comfortable with the shared terminology of that subject area. For instance, in computer science, the words "branching" or "loop" have specific meanings that are important to understand. The same thing is true in mathematics. In this section, we will review some common terminology. If you are comfortable with these ideas, you may wish to skim this section or refer back to it as needed.
